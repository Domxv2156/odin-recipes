Recipe for Brenda's Lasagna

A beautiful recipe created by Brenda who is a skilled home cook. The ingridients listed is suited perfectly for a family of 4.

Ingridients

  • pack of lasagna noodles
  • pound of lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper
  • spaghetti sauce
  • garlic

you get the point

Now take a look at the directions!

  1. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes
  2. Prehead oven to 350 degrees. Brown beef and season with salt and pepper, stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta. Alternate layers of noodles, meat and cheese
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
